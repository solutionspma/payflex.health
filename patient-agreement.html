Patient Agreement

Understanding PayFlex Health Services

Important: Please read this agreement carefully before using PayFlex Health services.

What PayFlex Health Is

PayFlex provides a technology platform that helps patients manage eligible out-of-pocket healthcare expenses offered by participating providers.

PayFlex is not a bank, lender, or insurance company and does not provide medical advice.

What This Means for You

No Credit Check

PayFlex does not perform credit checks or report to credit bureaus. Participation does not affect your credit score.

Not a Loan

PayFlex is not lending you money. You are funding your own healthcare expenses over time through automated payments.

Insurance Independent

Participation in PayFlex does not affect your insurance coverage, claims, or provider clinical decisions.

Your Responsibility

Patients remain responsible for all amounts agreed upon with their provider, regardless of PayFlex participation.

How It Works

  1. You select a payment schedule with your provider (3, 6, or 12 months)
  2. Your provider receives payment upfront for services rendered
  3. You make automated monthly payments via ACH from your bank account
  4. PayFlex charges a platform fee (5-15% depending on payment term)

Fees and Payment Terms

Platform Fee: You pay a platform fee for payment flexibility. The fee ranges from 5-15% based on your payment term length.

Example: $3,000 in healthcare expenses over 6 months with a 10% fee means you pay $3,300 total ($550/month for 6 months).

All fees are disclosed upfront before you agree to any payment schedule. No hidden charges.

Electronic Communications

By using PayFlex, you consent to:

  • Receive electronic records, agreements, and communications
  • Automated ACH withdrawals from your designated bank account
  • Email and SMS notifications about payments and account activity
  • Electronic signatures under the U.S. E-SIGN Act (15 U.S.C. §7001)

Payment Obligations

You agree to:

  • Make timely payments according to your selected schedule
  • Maintain sufficient funds in your bank account for ACH withdrawals
  • Notify PayFlex immediately of any bank account changes
  • Pay all agreed amounts regardless of insurance claims or provider outcomes

Missed Payments

If you miss a payment, PayFlex may charge a $25 failed payment fee. Multiple missed payments may result in collections activity and termination of your payment plan. You remain responsible for the full amount owed.

Privacy and Data Security

PayFlex uses bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information. We comply with HIPAA and all applicable data protection laws. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Termination

You may not terminate your payment plan early without paying the full remaining balance. PayFlex may terminate your account for violations of this agreement or failure to make payments.
